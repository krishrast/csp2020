PLTW 1.2.5 Project

This prject was inspired from the game Minion Rush, where a player navigates a minion through oncoming obstacles to go for as long as possible. We made a 2 dimensional version where boxes come at the minion and using the arrow keys, you need to move side to side and dodge the boxes.



PLTW 1.3.1 Project

The dodgeball game consists of a player and three balls which constantly bounce around in the box. The goal of the game is the avoid collision with the balls for as long as possible. The balls get faster and faster over time and the player has three lives. Once the player loses all three lives the game ends with the time they stayed alive displyed as the score.
